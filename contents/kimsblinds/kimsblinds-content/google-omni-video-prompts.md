# Kim's Blinds Google Omni Video Prompt Pack

Source brand: Kim's Blinds Security Doors Pty Ltd, Oak Flats NSW.

Use these prompts with captured site assets in `contents/kimsblinds/images` and production web assets in `public/brand/kims`.

## Global Direction

- Format: 8 seconds unless otherwise specified.
- Aspect ratios: generate 16:9 for desktop hero, 9:16 for mobile/social crops, and 1:1 for service cards.
- Style: photorealistic Australian coastal home, premium but practical, clean installation detail, bright natural light.
- Brand cues: glossy blue accent, warm orange sunlight, chrome edge highlights, soft glass reflections.
- Avoid: fake text, fake phone numbers, unreadable logos, people posing to camera, AI-looking hands, warped blinds, impossible architecture, cluttered rooms, over-dark grading.
